Automated Trading Bot

I built an automated trading bot in Python that streams live market data through a pandas-based pipeline, engineers technical-indicator features, and feeds them into a lightweight scikit-learn model to predict short-term price moves; whenever the model’s confidence clears a set threshold, the bot places paper trades via an API.
